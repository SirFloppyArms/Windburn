Windburn DEVO Team

Windburn Multi-Sport Academy’s DEVO team is a chance for new athletes 12-15 to get all the skills and experience in the different disciplines of triathlon to be ready to compete in KIDS OF STEEL up to CANADA GAMES. Training focuses on swim, bike and run, as well as combinations of these sports. The Devo team trains in a very welcoming training atmosphere with the Windburn Race Team.

The coaches are very fit and will challenge the athletes to achieve new levels of fitness though the summer. Many athletes train with the DEVO team during the summer to benefit from the incredible cross training effects on other seasonal sports (e.g., Ringette, hockey, volleyball, basketball, water polo). Benefits also include but are not limited to improved endurance, muscle balance and injury prevention.
