Welcome to the Windburn Race Team

The Windburn Race Team is a competitive-based triathlon team in Manitoba comprised of over 40 highly competitive athletes training year-round for Canada Games, Continental Cup and Elite Races, Age Group World Championships, 70.3, and Ironman competitions.

Our team creates an atmosphere that is fun, friendly, and intensely competitive. We bring together athletes of all ages who are focused on succeeding together while supporting one another. Whether you're aiming for podium finishes or personal bests, Windburn Race Team provides the structure, expertise, and community you need to achieve your triathlon goals.

Head Coach Dave Lipchen

Dave Lipchen brings decades of triathlon expertise and coaching excellence to lead the Windburn Race Team. With a deep understanding of elite-level training methodology, Dave designs individualized programs that take into account each athlete's strengths, weaknesses, and competition goals.

His approach combines scientific training principles with personalized attention, ensuring every athlete on the team receives the guidance they need to perform at their best. Dave's leadership creates an environment where competitive excellence and team camaraderie go hand in hand.

Meet Our Coaching Team

Our Training Philosophy

Swim

Technique refinement, speed work, and open water preparation. Developing efficient strokes and building endurance for competitive distances.

Bike

Power development, aerodynamic positioning, and tactical race simulation. Building the strength and skills needed for competitive cycling.

Run

Speed and stamina development with focus on proper form and injury prevention. Training for competitive pace and durability.

Cross Training

Cross country skiing, mountain biking, and trail running keep your body and mind fresh. These activities improve endurance, muscle balance, and prevent overuse injuries.

Training Commitment

Structured Group Sessions

The Windburn Race Team trains together through structured group sessions, building team cohesion and pushing each other toward excellence. Athletes are grouped by competitive level and training focus to ensure workouts are appropriately challenging and productive.

Training Volume: Athletes typically train 8–20 hours per week, with flexibility based on individual goals and competition schedules. Off-season and pre-competition phases have different training demands.

Joining the Windburn Race Team

Experience Level

Athletes should have foundational triathlon experience or demonstrated competency in multiple sports. Racing experience at the sprint or Olympic distance is typical.

Commitment

Athletes must be ready to commit 8–20 hours per week to training and embrace the structured training environment. Consistency and dedication are essential.

Attitude

We look for athletes who are competitive but also supportive of teammates. A positive, coachable attitude is fundamental to thriving on our team.

Goal Orientation

Whether aiming for podiums, personal bests, or World Championships, you should have clear triathlon goals and the drive to achieve them.
