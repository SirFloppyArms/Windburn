The History of RM Triathlon

The Riding Mountain Triathlon has been a cornerstone of the Manitoba triathlon community for over a decade. Nestled within the stunning landscape of Riding Mountain National Park, this race combines breathtaking natural beauty with a challenging course that tests athletes of all levels.

Founded with a vision to inspire athletes and celebrate outdoor sport, RM Triathlon has grown into one of Canada's premier triathlon events. Each year, hundreds of competitors from across North America gather to experience the unique blend of competitive racing and natural wonder that makes this race truly special.

From its humble beginnings to becoming a nationally recognized event, RM Triathlon remains committed to fostering a welcoming, inclusive, and competitive community of multi-sport athletes.

Frequently Asked Questions

When does registration open?

Registration typically opens 3 months before race day. Early bird pricing is available for the first 100 registrants.

Can I change my distance after registering?

Yes, you can change your distance up to 2 weeks before the race for a $25 fee. After that, no changes are permitted.

Is there a team registration option?

Yes! Team registration (minimum 3 people) receives a 15% discount. Teams must have representatives from at least 2 different organizations.

What is the refund policy?

Full refunds are available up to 4 weeks before race day. After that, transfers to future races are permitted.

What time does the race start?

Sprint distance athletes start at 7:00 AM, Olympic at 8:00 AM, and Half-Ironman at 6:00 AM. Each wave has a 5-minute interval.

Is the swim in open water or a pool?

The swim takes place in Clear Lake within Riding Mountain National Park. It's a beautiful open-water swim with mild current.

What is the bike course like?

The bike course is primarily on paved roads with some rolling hills. The terrain is moderate to challenging with spectacular views.

Are there aid stations on the course?

Yes, there are aid stations every 5-8km on the bike and every 2km on the run. All stations provide water, sports drinks, and nutrition.

How can I volunteer?

We're always looking for volunteers! Visit our volunteer sign-up page at least 4 weeks before race day. Volunteers receive a free event t-shirt and meals.

What volunteer roles are available?

Positions include: Course marshals, aid station support, timing/registration, transition area support, and parking coordination.

Do I get any benefits for volunteering?

Yes! Volunteers receive free entry to the next year's race (same distance), a volunteer t-shirt, meals during the event, and volunteer hours for community service.

Can I volunteer if I'm also racing?

Absolutely! We have volunteer positions available before and after the races (before the earliest start or after the last finisher).

Why should my company sponsor RM Triathlon?

RM Triathlon reaches 500+ athletes and their families annually. Sponsorship provides direct brand exposure to active, engaged community members.

What sponsorship levels are available?

We offer Platinum ($5,000+), Gold ($2,500), Silver ($1,000), and Bronze ($500) sponsorship packages with varying benefits.

What does a sponsorship include?

Benefits include: Logo placement on race materials, social media promotion, booth/tent space during race day, branded merchandise opportunities, and networking with our community.

How do I become a sponsor?

Contact our race director at info@rmtriathlon.ca with your interest. We'll send sponsorship package details and discuss custom opportunities for your organization.
