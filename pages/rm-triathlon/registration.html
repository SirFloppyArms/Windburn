Race Entry Overview

Riding Mountain Triathlon is designed for a broad range of athletes, from first-time multi-sport competitors through experienced triathletes racing longer and faster formats. The legacy site emphasized event access, athlete value, and clear registration rules. This page keeps the same purpose in a more concise format.

Typical Distance Options

  • Super Sprint
  • Sprint Triathlon
  • Olympic Triathlon
  • Duathlon and aquabike options when offered
  • Relay entries where available

Athlete Requirements

As reflected on the live site, athletes should expect to meet Triathlon Manitoba sanctioning requirements, including age eligibility and any required association membership or one-day race license.

What Registration Usually Includes

Race kit items, event support, post-race food, partner swag when available, and access to the full weekend event experience.

Registration Windows

Pricing typically increases over time. Early entry usually offers the best value and the widest range of available categories.

Important Policies

  • Race categories and pricing are subject to change each season.
  • Distance changes are usually only possible before a defined deadline and only if capacity allows.
  • Online registration is the main entry channel.
  • Weather, park conditions, and safety concerns can affect the event format or schedule.
  • Always review the current athlete guide before race weekend.
