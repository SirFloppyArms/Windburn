Volunteers

The legacy site correctly emphasized that the event depends on volunteers. This page keeps that message and makes the role breakdown easier to understand for first-time volunteers.

The Backbone of the Event

Riding Mountain Triathlon relies on a strong volunteer crew every year. From setup and registration to course safety and awards support, volunteers make the athlete experience possible and help create the welcoming atmosphere that racers remember.

If you want to support an athlete, give back to the community, or just be part of race weekend, volunteering is one of the most direct ways to help.

Why Volunteer?

  • Support a major Manitoba endurance event.
  • Be part of the race atmosphere without racing.
  • Help keep athletes safe and informed.
  • Connect with the triathlon community.

Pre-Race Setup

  • Set up transition area on Friday.
  • Install bike and run course signage.
  • Help prepare athlete-flow areas and support zones.

Race-Day Registration

  • Body marking
  • Athlete check-in support
  • Food table support
  • Cleanup and takedown assistance
  • Awards and prize support

Swim Support

  • Kayak or boat support
  • Swimmer marshaling at entry and exit points
  • Directional guidance in coordination with race officials

Bike Course Support

  • Course marshaling
  • Traffic and turn awareness support
  • Delivery or roaming assistance vehicles
  • Motorcycle support when available

Run Course Support

  • Course marshaling
  • Aid support and logistics help
  • Directional and safety support on course

General Event Help

  • Transition monitoring
  • Post-race cleanup
  • Recycling and site reset
  • Family and spectator information support

Volunteer Contact

If you want to help during race weekend, contact the team at info@windburnraceteam.com. Include your availability, whether you prefer pre-race or race-day support, and whether you have experience with watercraft, course marshaling, or athlete services.
