Race Weekend Planning

Accommodations

The old site listed places to stay around Riding Mountain and Clear Lake. This version keeps the same purpose, but organizes the options by lodging type so athletes and families can plan more quickly.

Make It a Full Weekend

Riding Mountain is more than a race venue. Many athletes arrive the day before, stay through the weekend, and turn race day into a family trip. Because accommodations in the area can fill quickly, it is smart to book early and confirm cancellation policies directly with each property.

Booking Tips

  • Book as early as possible for August race weekends.
  • Check drive time to transition and race-day parking access.
  • Ask about early breakfast or kitchenette options.
  • Confirm bike storage if you are travelling with expensive gear.
