Windburn Race Team

The Windburn Race Team is a competitive-based triathlon team in Manitoba comprised of athletes training for Elite and Age Group competitions. The team consists of over thirty competitive athletes representing all age groups. The team trains together 8-20 hours per week at venues including the University of Manitoba, Pan AM pool and Birds Hill Park.

Head Coach Dave Lipchen

Dave Lipchen brings decades of triathlon expertise and coaching excellence to lead the Windburn Race Team. With a deep understanding of elite-level training methodology, Dave designs individualized programs that take into account each athlete's strengths, weaknesses, and competition goals.

His approach combines scientific training principles with personalized attention, ensuring every athlete on the team receives the guidance they need to perform at their best. Dave's leadership creates an environment where competitive excellence and team camaraderie go hand in hand.

History

Founded in 2014, the Windburn Multi-Sport Academy provides a fun, friendly and competitive atmosphere for athletes aged 12 and up in a multi-sport environment.

Training Commitment

Structured Group Sessions

The Windburn Race Team trains together through structured group sessions, building team cohesion and pushing each other toward excellence. Athletes are grouped by competitive level and training focus to ensure workouts are appropriately challenging and productive.

Training Volume: Athletes typically train 8–20 hours per week, with flexibility based on individual goals and competition schedules. Off-season and pre-competition phases have different training demands.

What We Offer

  • Scientifically-designed training programs tailored to your goals
  • Access to group training sessions across all three disciplines
  • Expert coaching and technical feedback
  • Structured progression toward competition goals
  • Team atmosphere with athletes of similar competitive levels
  • Support resources including nutrition guidance and injury prevention
  • Competition recommendations and race preparation
