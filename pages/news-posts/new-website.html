New Website

A paraphrased archive version of the original announcement welcoming visitors to Windburn’s website.

April 2018 Category: News

Welcome to the Windburn website. The original post was a short launch note, and that spirit is kept here: a simple welcome message for athletes, families, and supporters looking for information about the race team, events, and program updates.

The site has evolved since then, but the goal remains the same: make it easier to find news, race information, team details, and registration resources in one place.
