Congratulations to Triathlon Coach Dave Lipchen

A paraphrased archive version of the original recognition post.

April 6, 2021 Category: News

This archive post recognizes Dave Lipchen for receiving the Dr. Jack Hunt Memorial Award through Sport Manitoba and Konica Minolta Canada. The original site presented it as a celebratory announcement and a public acknowledgment of his impact as a coach.

The broader significance is clear: the award reflects years of athlete development, technical leadership, and long-term contribution to the sport in Manitoba. It also reinforces Windburn’s identity as a program built around serious coaching rather than casual branding alone.
