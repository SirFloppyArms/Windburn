Training Schedule

This page recreates the practical schedule content from the old site in a cleaner format. Session times can shift seasonally, so athletes should always confirm the latest update with Coach Dave before attending a new session.

How Windburn Trains

Windburn group sessions are built around consistent swim, bike, and run work, with strength, pacing, and technical development layered in throughout the week. The exact weekly rhythm changes by season, but the goal remains the same: structured progression and a strong team environment.

The schedule below is based on the live training information published on the legacy site, reorganized into a format that is easier to scan on desktop and mobile.

Before You Attend

  • Confirm location details with the coaching staff.
  • Bring weather-appropriate gear for outdoor sessions.
  • Expect some sessions to be ability-grouped.
  • Email info@windburnraceteam.com for current updates.

Optional Elite Swim Add-On

The legacy site also referenced a limited-capacity stroke-development option for junior elite and elite athletes on select weekday mornings at Pan Am Pool. That remains the kind of add-on athletes should discuss directly with the coaching staff.

Training Volume

Most athletes train between 8 and 20 hours per week depending on age, experience, and goals. Group sessions are only part of the full program.
