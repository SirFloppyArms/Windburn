Windburn Multi-Sport Academy

Windburn Multi-Sport Academy Inc. develops athletes from their first multi-sport experience all the way up to elite competition. The atmosphere created for the race team is fun, friendly, and of course competitive. This format brings together athletes of all ages that are focused to succeed and support each other.

Windburn Multi-Sport Academy has two training groups “Windburn Race Team” and “Windburn DEVO Team”. Windburn’s training focuses on the fundamental sports of swim, bike, run, and branches out into the variations of each.

Cross training is a staple with the Windburn Multi-Sport Academy, and you will find yourself mixing in cross country skiing / mountain biking, and trail running to name a few keeping your body and mind fresh during the training sessions. Athletes focusing on other sports (e.g., hockey, volleyball, basketball, water polo) also benefit from cross training in this environment. Benefits include but are not limited to improved endurance, muscle balance and injury prevention.

Windburn Devo Team

Windburn Multi-Sport Academy introduced the DEVO (Development) team to bridge the gap between the youth and young adult races. The DEVO team provides a fun, friendly and competitive atmosphere for athletes aged 12-15 in a multi-sport environment. Training focuses on swim, bike, run, transitions, and the proper skill development required in each discipline.

Training also emphasizes building core strength. Athletes focusing on other sports (e.g., hockey, volleyball, basketball, water polo) also benefit from cross training in this environment. Benefits include but are not limited to improved endurance, muscle balance and injury prevention.
